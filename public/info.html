This is a simple seismograph simulator. Move your mouse up and down to see the seismograph in action.



How is this related to the topic: Our Changing Earth?

The earth's tectonic plates are constantly moving which changes the overall location of the tectonic plates and the things on it.
When these tectonic plates collide with one another, they cause an earthquake.
The vibrations caused by tectonic plate movements are recorded by a device called as a 'seismograph'.
Our website is a simulation of a seismograph that records an 'earthquake' made by the red box's movement.
